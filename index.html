Common Questions

What is Reiki?

Reiki is a gentle yet powerful energy healing technique that works on the emotional, physical, mental, and spiritual levels.

We all carry a universal life force energy. When that energy becomes blocked or stagnant, it can lead to imbalance, fatigue, emotional heaviness, or disconnection from ourselves.

Reiki restores the natural flow of energy in your body by gently channeling this life force through the hands of the practitioner. The process is subtle, non-invasive, and deeply nurturing. It does not interfere with medical or psychological treatments — in fact, it often supports and enhances them.

You do not have to believe in anything for Reiki to work — your body already knows how to receive.

Are your sessions online or in-person?

My sessions are held online only, and the effect is just as powerful as being in the same room — in fact, many people find it even better.

Reiki flows beyond time and space, and since deep relaxation is essential for the energy to work, there is no better place to receive than from the comfort and safety of your own home.

How does a distance Reiki session work?

In Reiki, energy is not bound by time or space. Through intention and traditional Reiki symbols, healing energy can be channeled across distance, connecting directly to your energetic field, wherever you are.

We will meet via a live 1-on-1 video call on Google Meet or WhatsApp. After booking, you will receive a private link to a preparation folder with:

✧ Simple instructions
✧ Optional calming music
✧ Suggestions to create a supportive space before your session

Your session begins with a short check-in and a brief breathwork practice to open the body and energy channel. You will then rest with eyes closed as Reiki flows where it is needed.

At the end, there is space to gently return and share any experiences. I will send you a written follow-up afterward with intuitive insights to help integrate the session.

What can Reiki help me with?

Reiki meets you exactly where you are, helping soften, release, and realign what is ready to shift.

⟡ Chakra balancing & energetic realignment
⟡ Sleep issues, tension & restlessness
⟡ Heartbreak, grief, loss & emotional pain
⟡ Spiritual disconnection or lack of clarity
⟡ Deepening intuition & soul connection
⟡ Clearing stagnant or heavy energy
⟡ Energy fatigue, burnout & spiritual blockages
⟡ Life transitions, endings & awakenings

What will I feel during a session?

Everyone experiences Reiki differently. Some people feel warmth, tingling, deep relaxation, or emotional release, while others may even fall asleep. You might notice shifts right away or gently in the days following the session.

It is important not to expect a specific sensation. Reiki works beyond the mind’s control, and the energy always flows where it is needed most, even if the experience feels subtle in the moment.

How many sessions do I need?

Reiki works by removing layers step by step.

One session can bring relief and clarity, but to experience deeper and more stable energetic shifts, it usually takes 8–10 sessions. This allows the healing to go deeper and fully integrate over time.
